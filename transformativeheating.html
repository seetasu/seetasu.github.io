Transformative
Heating

Heating system designed for transformative home

Role in the group

Concept generation, prototyping

Duration

02.2016 - 04.2017
(2 weeks)

Scope

Concept Design

Brief

Ever imagined what you could do with your heating system? Instead of radiators standing by the wall, what if we can manipulate the warm as the way we want it?
Based on the notion of 'Savouring Warmth', we innovated on the heating system and created two concepts that allows people to savour the nice feeling of warmth with more control and fun.

Concept 1

This concept provides a new exploratory way to change the level of warmth by landscaping rice. The heat source is hidden under the rice landscape. One can either release the heat by creating open spaces (where more heat can go through), or cover up heat by moving the rice.

Concept 2

This concept is an adjustable warm spot. The warmth will go down when people come near to it and. It creates a nice atmosphere for people standing around it having a conversation and also gives them warm hands.
