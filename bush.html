Concept

Growing Habits is an innovative data visualization on personal health. In our daily life, one doesn’t see data. They don’t see how much calories they eat and what is the temperature of air surrounding them. Instead, all the daily activities create experiences and unique feelings, such as “I need my warmest coat today”. So in the concept, we bypassed raw number, and instead, visualizd experiential data.

Users log in their preference on given tasks, and they generates the data in the interactions, which is distinguished from personal health data generated by sensors. They have power over the visualization of the data. Users will gain more concrete perception of their decisions, and grow healthier lifestyle gradually.
